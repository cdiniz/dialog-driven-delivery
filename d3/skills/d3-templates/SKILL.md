# D3 Standard Templates

This skill's `references/` directory contains the canonical templates for D3 artifacts. The `/d3:init` command copies them into `.d3/templates/` in the user's project, where they can be inspected and customized.

## Templates

- `references/feature-product-spec.md` — Product requirements and business context
- `references/feature-tech-spec.md` — Implementation details and technical decisions
- `references/user-story.md` — Individual implementable user stories (Given-When-Then)
- `references/adr.md` — Architectural decision records (MADR v4)
- `references/meeting-transcript.md` — Structured meeting transcript capture
